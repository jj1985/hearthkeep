## Pantheon

- **Thaen the Forge** — craft, oath, hearth
- **Ysmir of the Tide** — voyage, fortune, the deep
- **Velis Brightleaf** — growth, song, healing
- **Morrun Blackveil** — shadow, secrets, the long path
- **Torath the Unburned** — dragonfire, ash, war (deity, not dragon — distinct)
- **Sennari** — chance, gambling, the dice's edge

## Factions

- **Coastreach Crown** — trade-house knights and crown coin
- **Black Bastion Consortium** — guildmasters who lend a price for everything
- **Canopyhall Council** — elven keepers of the canopy and the long memory
- **Kaeldur Clans** — dwarven smiths and rune-wardens of the deep stone
- **Free Companies** — mercenary warbands

## NPCs

- **Master Vrak** — Forge Master at the Hearthkeep villa
- **Old Bren** — Tavernkeeper, runs the rumor pool
- **Snikkit the Lucky** — goblin defector who runs the Gambling Den (basement of the villa)
- **Sir Alric** — fallen Crown knight whose blade is a quest hook

**HEARTHKEEP — of the Sundered Realms.**
